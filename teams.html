ALUMNI CORNER

Time to meet the Team and Alumni

We were not always as what we are today. What you are seeing is a fruitful result of contionous hardwork of a lot of IIT Guwahati students
thoughout the years. Founded in 2014 we have been striving for success ever since.

Have a look and connect with us and our former leaders.
This is all we have and this is all we are. They are FEC

FEC QUANT

Quantitative Analysis Team FEC

Started with the motive to improve the quantitative analysis culture in the college. Today we are one of the biggest and the most productive
team in Technical Borad IIT Guwahati with Multiple Inter IIT Medals Including Bronze in Inter IIT 2022.

What we usually focus on:

1. Portfolio Optimization 2. Option Pricing and Strategies 3. Trading strategies and Backtesting

FEC CAPITAL

Fund Management Team FEC

Started in 2019 - 2020 the team is still in its growth phase. But our Growth phase is something what other teams might call exceptional,
Finance & Economics Club IIT Guwahati is the only Technical club in the country to have student run Mutual Fund

What we usually focus on:

1. Investment 2. Portfolio Management 3. Fundamental Analysis 4. Valuation and Foundation of Finance

FEC DE - FI

Decentralized Finance & Blockchain Team FEC

Blockchain, NFT’s, Crypto has the hottest topic of discussion for the last 2 years and with focus on the same topic FEC De-Fi was founded in
2022. The Team is relatively amateur as compared to others but the hunger for growth and learning remains unmatched.

What we usually focus on:

1. Solidity Programming 2. NFT & Crypto 3. Blockchain Development & Backtesting

How do we work?

We at Finance & Economics Club, IIT Guwahati are divided in three teams named: FEC Quant, FEC Capital, FEC De-Fi. Teams work on multiple
projects throughout the year and also participate in various competitions all over the globe.

Mind having a deeper look at what each team offers?
This is how we usually roll. This is how we strive for the best.

FEC QUANT

Quantitative Analysis Team FEC

Started with the motive to improve the quantitative analysis culture in the college. Today we are one of the biggest and the most
productive team in Technical Borad IIT Guwahati with Multiple Inter IIT Medals Including Bronze in Inter IIT 2022.

What we usually focus on:

1. Portfolio Optimization 2. Option Pricing and Strategies 3. Trading strategies and Backtesting

FEC CAPITAL

Fund Management Team FEC

Started in 2019 - 2020 the team is still in its growth phase. But our Growth phase is something what other teams might call
exceptional, Finance & Economics Club IIT Guwahati is the only Technical club in the country to have student run Mutual Fund.

What we usually focus on:

1. Investment 2. Portfolio Management 3. Fundamental Analysis 4. Valuation and Foundation of Finance

FEC DE - FI

Decentralized Finance & Blockchain Team FEC

Blockchain, NFT’s, Crypto has the hottest topic of discussion for the last 2 years and with focus on the same topic FEC De-Fi was
founded in 2022. The Team is relatively amateur as compared to others but the hunger for growth and learning remains unmatched.

What we usually focus on:

1. Solidity Programming 2. NFT & Crypto 3. Blockchain Development & Backtesting

FEC SOCIALS

Social Media & Content Team FEC

The club lays a lot of focus on improving the financial literacy in the society and to reach out to the people nothing better than
social comes to our mind. With the same thinking we are now available on near about all social media websites including Instagram, LinkedIn & Medium.

What we usually focus on:

1. Educational Posts on Instagram & LinkedIn 2. Regular Blogs on Medium 3. InFocus - Our Flagship Stock Read

About the Course

Winter Consulting is a flagship course conducted by Consulting and Analytics Club every year, training thousands of students and working professionals to start their career in management consulting or get a general understanding of the business world.
With the availability of numerous paid and free courses across the internet, it becomes overwhelming for students trying to learn business concepts like economics, finance, marketing and Consulting. To demtsify the process, we curated a specialised course from 20+ websites, a create a simplified path for our learners to follow within 4 weeks.
The course will include diverse range of content in the form of video lectures, blogs, books, specialised articles and website links. Extensive practice in the form of daily questions, weekly contests and numerous assignments will be conducted to ensure a firm grasp of the concepts.
We are confident that the course will be a memorable journey for all kind of learners and there is something new for everyone!!
So don't procastinate and register to make full use of this rare opportunity available absolutely free of cost
The course is conducted by Consulting and Analytics Club of IIT Guwahati in the winters, training more than 500+ students each year. The online course was started to train the students at IIT Guwahati campus but eventually spread out to different Universities across India.

What do we do for the society?

We at Finance & Economics Club, IIT Guwahati believe that finance is not all about money but it is one of the most crucial part of our lives.

We try our best in removing this stigma by conducting workshops, courses, events and a lot more

For the past few years the club has conducted more than 10 industry level courses and workshops with participation from more than 100 institutions all over the globe.

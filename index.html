How do we work?

We at Finance & Economics are divided into three teams: FEC Quant, FEC Capital, and FEC De-Fi. The team works on multiple projects throughout the year, conducting and participating in various competitions. All the teams have their history of achievements and projects. Social empowerment

What do we do for the society?

We at Finance & Economics Club, IIT Guwahati believe that Finance is not all about money but it is one of the most crucial part of our lives. We try our best in removing this stigma by conducting workshops, courses, events and a lot more Have a look at some of our flagship events.

Finance Plus

An initiative taken in 2021-2022 to increase the financial iteracy among the students of age 18-24. Finance Plus is designed in such a way that the course can be opted by anyone who finds finance interesting.

MnAnalyse

MnAnalyse is the India's only and largest Mergers & Equation Based Case Competition. Started in 2022 it attracts teams from more than 100 institutes including all major IIMs, IITs and other institutes of National importance.

Financial Derivatives

Financial Derivatives & Option Pricing is one of the flagship courses of Finance & Economics Club, IIT Guwahati. The course is entirely focused on option pricing, Delta Hedging and Derivatives.

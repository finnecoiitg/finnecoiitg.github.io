How do we work?

Since 2014 the club has been trying its very best to serve society and the IIT Guwahati students in the best possible manner, and while doing the same, we started segmenting our work specifically in multiple teams named: FEC Quant, FEC Capital, and FEC De-Fi. All the teams work rigorously throughout the year on multiple projects. And even participate in various competitions all over the globe.

What do we do for the society?

We at Finance & Economics Club, IIT Guwahati believe that Finance is not all about money but it is one of the most crucial part of our lives. We try our best in removing this stigma by conducting workshops, courses, events and a lot more.

For the past few years the club has conducted more than 10 industry level courses and workshops with participation from more than 100 institutions all over the globe.

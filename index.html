INTRODUCING YOU TO THE CLUB

How do we work?

We at Finance & Economics are divided into three teams: FEC Quant, FEC Capital, and FEC De-Fi. The team works on multiple projects throughout the year, conducting and participating in various competitions. All the teams have their history of achievements and projects. Please have a look at their pages.

Social empowerment

What do we do for the society?

We at Finance & Economics Club, IIT Guwahati believe that Finance is not all about money but it is one of the most crucial part of our lives. We try our best in removing this stigma by conducting workshops, courses, events and a lot more Have a look at some of our flagship events.

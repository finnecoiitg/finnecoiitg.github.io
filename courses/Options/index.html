What is an Option Derivative?
Options are financial instruments that are derivatives based on the value of underlying securities such as stocks. You bet on a stock by saying your price when it expires.

In this 3-week course, you will learn about options and their strategies. This is a certified course by Finance and Economics Club, and you would be graded based on weekly assignments and quizzes.

About the Course

Intricate concepts abound in the business world, all of which have near-infinite applications when combined. We diligently curated a selection of courses from dozens of websites and created a three-week study route for our community to follow.
This FREE certification course, which begins on March 28, aims to break these principles into a manageable but thorough week-based format, supported by a mentor-led peer-learning group.

This course will have multiple certifications:
Certificate 1: If you will complete all the weeks Assignments and also submit the project.
Certificate 2: If you will only submit the Weekly Assignments but do not submit the project.
Certificate 3: If you will only submit the project but do not submit the Weekly Assignments.

Duration 3 weeks
Pre-requisite None
Projects 2
